About Enduro Capital

Investing in the Heart of Dutch Logistics

Enduro Capital is a private equity firm dedicated to fostering growth in small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) within the Dutch logistics sector. We specialize in acquiring and investing in logistics companies that own real estate assets integral to their operations.

Our mission is to empower these businesses to reach new heights by providing the capital, expertise, and strategic support they need to thrive in a competitive market.

Our Approach

Strategic Partnerships for Sustainable Growth

At Enduro Capital, we believe in building lasting partnerships with the companies we invest in. Our approach is centered around understanding the unique challenges and opportunities within the logistics industry.

  • We provide not just capital but also strategic guidance.

  • Our team works closely with management to enhance operational efficiency.

  • We focus on long-term growth and value creation.

Investment Criteria

Seeking Companies with Growth Potential

Industry Focus

Logistics companies in the Netherlands, including transportation, fulfillment, and warehousing.

Asset Ownership

Companies owning real estate assets integral to their operations.

Financial Metrics

Minimum €2M in revenues and €300K EBITDA.

Ownership

Seeking majority stake acquisitions for active strategic involvement.
