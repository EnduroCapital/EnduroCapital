About Enduro Capital

Your Partner in Logistics Growth

Enduro Capital is a search fund founded by industry experts Gianni Vulcano and Martijn de Blaey. A search fund is an investment vehicle designed to help entrepreneurs acquire and grow a single privately held company. We focus on identifying promising SMEs in the Dutch logistics sector, providing them with strategic capital and hands-on expertise to drive growth.

With our deep understanding of the logistics industry and financial markets, we aim to create long-term value for both investors and the companies we partner with. Our approach combines rigorous analysis, strategic planning, and collaborative leadership to unlock a company's full potential.

Our Approach

A Hands-On Strategy for Success

At Enduro Capital, we believe in building lasting partnerships based on trust, transparency, and shared goals. Our approach involves:

  • Identifying High-Potential Companies: We search for SMEs with strong fundamentals and growth potential in the logistics sector.
  • Acquiring Majority Stakes: By acquiring majority ownership, we can actively participate in strategic decision-making and drive meaningful change.
  • Providing Strategic Capital: We offer not just financial investment but also strategic guidance to help companies scale effectively.
  • Hands-On Management: Our team works closely with company leadership to implement operational improvements and growth initiatives.

Investment Criteria

Seeking Companies with Growth Potential

We are actively seeking partnerships with companies that align with our investment philosophy. Our focus is on businesses that demonstrate strong potential for growth and share our commitment to excellence.

If your company meets the following criteria, we would be interested in exploring a partnership:

Industry Focus

Logistics companies in the Netherlands, including transportation, fulfillment, and warehousing.

Asset Ownership

Companies owning real estate assets integral to their operations.

Financial Metrics

Minimum €2M in revenues and €300K EBITDA.

Ownership

Seeking majority stake acquisitions for active strategic involvement.
